equiMe

Invest Human.

equiMe started as a passion project born from fantasy baseball and early investing. The idea was simple: what if you could invest directly in someone you believe in? No debt, no gatekeepers. A personal asset platform where athletes, creators, and entrepreneurs can issue shares in their future brand value, and early believers participate in the upside.

As Founder and CEO, I designed and built every layer of equiMe from scratch. The platform, the infrastructure, the financial models, the legal framework. Read the full story on the equiMe blog.

Pre-seed. MVP in active development. Targeting the $1B+ NIL market and the $500B+ creator economy.

About Me

Identity and Access Management (IAM) engineer and technology evangelist at Koch Industries, based in Austin, TX. I specialize in SSO architecture, Identity Governance (IGA), cloud automation, and full-stack development.

I design and implement enterprise authentication systems — from migrating PingFederate to PingOne SaaS, to building scalable identity pipelines across AWS, Azure, and GCP. My work bridges security and developer experience, leveraging Terraform, REST APIs, Python, and AI-assisted engineering tools.

Outside of my day job, I founded EquiMe — a blockchain-based technology venture built on Go, Python/Flask, and React/TypeScript. I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of identity, security, and decentralized systems.

I graduated early from Missouri State University with a double major in IT Infrastructure & Cybersecurity, and I bring the same discipline to everything I build.
